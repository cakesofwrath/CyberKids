﻿What happens if you want to make your sprite disappear? Do you move your sprite to off the screen? When you do this, you can actually still see the edge of your sprite on screen. That doesn’t look so great. So, now you’re going to learn how to make your character disappear, and reappear! You’re also going to learn how to make your program wait before moving on to the next command. This is useful for when you want to move your sprite one place, then wait a couple seconds, then have it move again somewhere else.
