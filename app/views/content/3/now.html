So now that you know how to make your sprite move and talk, why don’t you try combining the two? Some ideas: have it walk around the screen and tell a story, or walk back and forth and tell dumb knock knock jokes! But you don’t have to do those - that’s the great part about Scratch: you can try anything you want, so go on ahead and experiment, and move on to the next lesson when you feel ready.
