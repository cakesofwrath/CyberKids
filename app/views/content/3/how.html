As you learned last lesson, you must have the “When (Green Flag) clicked” button to start any program. So let’s do that first. If you forgot where it is, that’s okay, it’s located in the label “Events”.

Next, in order to have your sprite talk, go to the label “Looks”.

##1.png##

The first 4 buttons allows for your sprite to talk/think whatever text you type into the block. There are two types of these blocks: the blocks with times on them will only appear as long as the time entered, and the blocks without times will last forever. Go on ahead and choose any type of this block to put in your script.
