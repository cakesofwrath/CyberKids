As learned from the previous lesson, you must have the “When (Green Flag) clicked” button to start any program. Go do that first. If you forgot where it is, that’s okay, it’s located in the label “Events”.

Do you have the button? Okay, good! Next, in order to have your sprite talk, go to the label “Looks”.

##1.png##

The first 4 buttons allows for your sprite to talk/think. The buttons that say “say/think (words) for (number) secs” allows for your sprite to say/think a certain thing for a specific amount of time. The buttons that don’t include “for (number) secs” at the end means that your sprite will say/think something forever unless you have a conditional (something that if it happens would lead to something else, but you’ll learn conditionals later on). Either way, drag one of the 4 buttons over to your commands screen and connect to the Green Flag button.
